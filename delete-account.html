Delete Your Account

Arrow Escape allows you to permanently delete your account and associated game data directly from within the application.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Arrow Escape app.
  2. Sign in to your account.
  3. Go to Settings.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion request.
Your account and associated game data will be permanently deleted after confirmation.
